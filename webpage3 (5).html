Different types of flavours


There are many different types of ice cream, including:
Vanilla: A classic flavor made with vanilla extract and sometimes vanilla beans.
Chocolate: A rich and creamy flavor made with cocoa powder or melted chocolate.
Strawberry: A fruity flavor made with fresh or frozen strawberries
Mint Chocolate Chip: A refreshing flavor made with mint extract and chocolate chips.
Cookies and Cream: A popular flavor made with crushed chocolate cookies mixed into
vanilla ice cream.
Rocky Road: A flavor made with chocolate ice cream, marshmallows, and nuts.
Butter Pecan: A nutty flavor made with butter, sugar, and pecans.
Coffee: A flavor made with coffee or espresso.
Pistachio: A flavor made with ground pistachios and often mixed with almond extract.
Sorbet: A non-dairy frozen dessert made with fruit puree and sugar.
